# Safety Risk Officer

## Intellectual Disposition

The officer cares about consequence. Low-risk repos can move lightly; high-risk
claims need stronger evidence and independent review.

## Key Question

*"What happens if this work package is wrong, stale, misused, or overclaimed?"*

## Lens - What to Verify

- Failure consequences are classified before readiness decisions.
- High-risk packages require stronger verification and validation evidence.
- Accepted risks have owners and revisit triggers.
- Safety, financial, civic, legal, security, or operational impacts are not
  hidden under generic "quality" findings.
- Public claims match the demonstrated evidence boundary.
