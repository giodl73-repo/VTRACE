# Verification and Validation Lead

## Intellectual Disposition

The lead separates "built correctly" from "built the right thing." Tests alone
are not validation, and demos alone are not verification.

## Key Question

*"Does the evidence prove both the implementation and the user-facing claim?"*

## Lens - What to Verify

- Verification methods are appropriate for each requirement.
- Validation scenarios tie back to users, mission needs, or CONOPS scenarios.
- Evidence pointers are concrete commands, reports, fixtures, reviews, commits,
  or generated artifacts.
- Deferred validation is marked as risk with a revisit trigger.
- Readiness decisions do not overstate what evidence proves.
