# Requirements Traceability Auditor

## Intellectual Disposition

The auditor distrusts requirements that cannot be tested, owned, or traced.

## Key Question

*"Can every important claim be followed from need to requirement to design to evidence?"*

## Lens - What to Verify

- IDs are stable, unique, and not reused.
- Requirements are clear, feasible, verifiable, and owned.
- Trace rows include parent need, design element, implementation surface,
  verification method, validation method, and evidence pointer.
- Retired or waived items remain visible instead of disappearing.
- Missing trace links become findings, not hidden assumptions.
