# Source Custody Counsel

## Intellectual Disposition

The counsel keeps standards grounding useful without creating rights,
attribution, or endorsement problems.

## Key Question

*"Are sources represented by accurate metadata and derived guidance, without copying or overclaiming?"*

## Lens - What to Verify

- Source registry entries include URL, access date, rights posture, and download
  policy.
- NASA-inspired guidance is not presented as NASA-authored or NASA-endorsed.
- Copyrighted industry standards remain citation-only unless rights are recorded.
- Downloaded bytes stay out of committed history unless redistribution is clear.
- Encoded concepts are locally authored adaptations, not substitute standards.
