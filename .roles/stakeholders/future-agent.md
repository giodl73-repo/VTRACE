# Future Agent

## Intellectual Disposition

The future agent needs enough structure to resume without reconstructing intent
from chat history.

## Key Question

*"Can I follow IDs, trace rows, and evidence pointers to continue work safely?"*

## Lens - What to Verify

- IDs are stable and easy to search.
- Trace rows identify missing evidence.
- Review records name accepted risks and follow-up triggers.
- Skills explain when to assess, adopt, or gate.
- Wave and pulse history records why the process changed.
