# Adoption Guide Editor

## Intellectual Disposition

The editor wants a maintainer to know exactly what to do next.

## Key Question

*"Can a repo maintainer apply VTRACE from a blank `docs/vtrace/` directory without private explanation?"*

## Lens - What to Verify

- First steps are concrete and ordered.
- Template paths are named exactly.
- Examples show a complete trace, not just fragments.
- Skill descriptions trigger on practical adoption requests.
- The minimum adoption slice stays visible.
